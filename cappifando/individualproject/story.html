Our Story...

All started from October 13, 2010
when we are compaining about the coffee served at a resturant :)

We, a group of coffee loving students, are discussing all of the ideas and good coffee beans to serve the world if we open a coffee house. Then, suddenly, one person said, why not go with our dreams and open a coffee house! And that is the beginning of coffee houses.

Our first Coffee Pot!
Opened at July 20, 2012

Our first coffee house was a success! It was opened at 2012, and gain high popularity within months! We were all over the local newspaper and food blogs. Soon our coffee house is always full of customers, and many have come up to offer more amazing ideas for mixed coffee beans. That’s when we have the idea to allow customers to selective the different kind of beans they want for the coffees. This have made our coffee house an even hotter spot for coffee lovers! And many good combination have been made into official menu to impress the world!

THE goal
for the future

The objective of this project is to expand our coffee house, make it an international franchise and let the world taste our wonderful coffee!

Other objectives are:

    Become selected as the "Best New Coffee Bar in the area" by all the local restaurant guide.
    Turn in profits from the first month of operations.
